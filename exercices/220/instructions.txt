Print every prime numbers in a range
====================================

Introduces: list, strings, functions.

Print every prime number in the range [10000;10050], separated by
comas and spaces.

You may use your is_prime function like this :

import exo0
print exo0.is_prime(999)

It will look like:
1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6

You may use a list, and str.join
